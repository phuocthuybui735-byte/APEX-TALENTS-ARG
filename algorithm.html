Neural Resonance Matching (神经共振匹配)

CORE MODULE I

Synaptic Mapping (突触映射)

我们将候选人的思维模式转化为 1024 维向量，捕捉其专业技能、思维方式、行为模式与价值观的精确数学表达。每个神经元节点代表一个认知特征，节点间的连接强度反映了特征间的关联关系。

We convert candidate thinking patterns into 1024-dimensional vectors, capturing precise mathematical expressions of their professional skills, thinking styles, behavioral patterns, and values. Each neuron node represents a cognitive feature, and the connection strength between nodes reflects the correlation between features.

Cognitive Compatibility Score (认知兼容性评分)

通过毫秒级突触对齐计算，系统在多维向量空间中寻找完美匹配。该算法不仅考虑当前岗位需求，还预测了未来 3-5 年的发展潜力与公司文化融合度。

Through millisecond-level synaptic alignment calculations, the system finds perfect matches in the multidimensional vector space. The algorithm not only considers current position requirements but also predicts future development potential and company culture integration over 3-5 years.

Predictive Behavioral Modeling (预测性行为建模)

CORE MODULE II

Micro-expression Tracking (微表情追踪)

通过分析 0.1 秒内的瞳孔变化、面部微表情与肢体语言，系统可精确识别欺骗行为与真实情绪反应。这种技术已在国家安全领域得到验证，准确率高达 98.7%。

By analyzing pupil dilation, facial micro-expressions, and body language within 0.1 seconds, the system can accurately identify deception and true emotional responses. This technology has been validated in national security applications with an accuracy rate of 98.7%.

Psychometric Profiling (心理测量侧写)

结合认知测试与情景模拟，系统构建完整的心理测量模型。通过对回答时间间隔、选择模式与犹豫频率的分析，预测员工未来 5 年的忠诚度、压力阈值与离职概率。

Combining cognitive tests and scenario simulations, the system constructs a complete psychometric model. By analyzing response time intervals, selection patterns, and hesitation frequencies, it predicts employee loyalty, stress thresholds, and turnover probability over the next 5 years.

Anomaly Detection (异常检测)

系统会自动标记不符合企业标准的行为模式，如过度思考、独立判断倾向过强或情绪波动过大。这些候选人将被分配到优化池进行认知修正。
